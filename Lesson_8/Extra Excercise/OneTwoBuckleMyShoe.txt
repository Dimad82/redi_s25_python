One, two,
Buckle my shoe;
Three, four,
Knock at the door;
Five, six,
Pick up sticks;
Seven, eight,
Lay them straight;
Nine, ten,
A clucking hen;
Eleven, twelve,
Dig and delve;
Thirteen, fourteen,
Crows reporting;
Fifteen, sixteen,
Kids in the kitchen;
Seventeen, eighteen,
Mom's a-waiting;
Nineteen, twenty,
Kids' plates empty.
